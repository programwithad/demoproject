🚀 What is Paradox Travel Connect?

Paradox Travel Connect is a platform built exclusively for IIT Madras BS students attending Paradox 2025. As thousands of students travel to Chennai from across India, this app helps you connect with fellow travelers sharing the same city, train, or station.

🚆 Why This App?

Traveling alone can be overwhelming — especially across states. Many students are looking for:

  • Companions to travel with
  • People from their own city
  • Others taking the same train or boarding from the same station

This app bridges that gap.

🔗 Key Features

💬
Group Chats
Train & City-wise group chats to talk with fellow travelers
🎯
Travel Matchmaking
Find people on the same train, station, or coach
📱
Direct Messaging
Individual chat to coordinate travel plans
🔐
Verified Community
Email-based login with official IITM BS emails only

🔒 Privacy & Security

We prioritize user safety and data privacy:

  • Seat and coach details are visible only to your co-passengers (same train)
  • Sensitive information like PNR numbers or ticket copies is never shared
  • Phone numbers are not displayed to any user
  • The app is restricted to verified IITM BS email logins only

💡 Built By Students, For Students

This project is a student-led initiative created by IITM BS students to solve a real travel coordination challenge for Paradox 2025. We're constantly improving — your suggestions are welcome!
